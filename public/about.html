About Me

Let me tell you a little bit about myself...

I guess this is me now

I spent most of my adult life working as a sound and lighting engineer in the UK. The part I loved the most about this job was taking 3 trucks full of equipment and turning them into a fully functioning event. From programming the sound, and the lighting rig, to delivering the best show possible. Plus I got to meet loads of amazing people along the way. A couple years back I decided that it would be nice to actually get some regular sleep hours, as I am not 20 years old anymore. That is an unimaginable goal in the music industry. I turned my attention towards programming and I fell in love with it. I've always felt lucky that the education system failed to beat the love for learning from me.

In programming you never run out of things to learn. Live sound and lighting is basically all aobut problem solving in high pressure environment. You have very limited time to make a thousand moving pieces work together. You have to adopt the 'keep your head cool' attitude. That's something that really helped me when learning programming. If you're able to tough it out through the frustration of development, the end result is always worth it.
